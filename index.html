About Me

I am a hardworking, self starting, Junior Web Developer with 5+ years of design experience working with a variety of many local businesses creating branding material, logos, and design templates. Although I am new to the tech industry I have a strong passion for learning and an incredible desire to build and design user friendly, and highly efficient web applications.

Currently employed as a Junior Web Developer at Disability Rights California, a non-profit law firm in Sacramento. While working along side my communications team, I created, updated, and tested various different web pages for public events, resources, and legal documents. I helped increase work productivity by 25% while simultaneously improving website traffic by implementing signup pages and invite emails to promote all web events using HTML, JavaScript and CSS in the Drupal framework.

Outside of work you can find me working on a few projects for school while also gaining real world experience designing and building websites for local businesses. As of late I have been working on a donation site for a non-profit horse rescue built in Python with the Django framework, and an eCommerce site built with Next.js React and Sanity.
